Little Story

The White Heaven features air-conditioned rooms with cable flat-screen TV in the New Town district of Kolkata. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi. The hotel has family rooms. At the hotel, rooms are equipped with a balcony. With a private bathroom, rooms at The White Heaven also boast a city view..
